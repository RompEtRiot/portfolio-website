About

My Story

A formal design education, opened the door to remote freelance opportunities and a full-time position at Toronto advertising agency. My passion for building tools for every-day life, led me to pursue UX full-time.
I consider UX to be detective work at its core. Our role as UX designers is to use the clues at our disposal to unite the desires of clients, teams, and the people who use our products. I currently work with a team of developers to connect patients struggling with obesity, with registered dietitians.
